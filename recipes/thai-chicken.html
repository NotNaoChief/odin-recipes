Sweet and Succulent Chicken with a Kick

  • Feeds: 2
  • Prep Time: 30 minutes to 48 hours
  • Cook Time: 25-30 minutes

Instructions

  • Sambal Oelek: 1/8 cup
  • Mae Ploy: 3/8 cup
  • Chicken Breast: 1 lb - 2-4 Breasts Depending on Size
  • Scallions: 3-4 stalks

Utensils

  • Cutting Board
  • Chef Knife
  • Fork/Tongs
  • Measuring Cup: 1/8 cup
  • Small Whisk
  • Baking Pan (Rimmed) or Baking Dish
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Food Safe Thermometer
  • Small Mixing Bowl (Preferably With a Lid)
  • Container to Marinade Chicken In (Only if Mixing Bowl Has No Lid)

Steps

  1. Add Sambal Oelek and Mae Ploy to the mix bowl and whisk until mixed thoroughly.
  2. Rinse scallions, cut stalks at the greens, throwing away the whites, then slice the greens of the scallions, setting aside for later.
  3. Clean chicken breasts on the cutting board by removing excess fat, and the center tendon, with your knife.
  4. Add chicken to the marinade and coat evenly.
  5. Add the lid and refrigerate. If in a rush allow at least 30 minutes, other wise it's best after 24-48 hours.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  7. When oven is at temp, add the chicken to the baking pan/dish. Be sure to pour your marinade all over the chicken.
  8. Cover the pan/dish with aluminum foil.
  9. Place in oven for 15 minutes.
  10. After 15 minutes, pull the chicken from the oven, and peel back the aluminum, being careful not to rip it. Using a fork/tongs flip the chicken, recover it with the aluminum foil and return to the oven for another 10-15 mins.
  11. When the time has passed check the temperature insuring its over 165 (Preferably 169-171 for most succulent) degrees fahrenheit.
  12. Slice the chicken thin, then sprinkle with scallions.
