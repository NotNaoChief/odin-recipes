Thai Chicken

Photo by icon0com from PxHere

Description

This Thai Chicken recipe is sweet with a bit of a kick! We will be combining the spicy flavor of Sambal Oelek paste with the wonderful sweetness that is Mae Ploy sweet chili sauce, into a wonderful marinade for succulent baked chicken!

Ingredients

  • Sambal Oelek: 1/8 cup
  • Mae Ploy: 3/8 cup
  • Chicken Breast: 1 lb - 2-4 breasts depending on size
  • Scallions: 3-4 stalks
